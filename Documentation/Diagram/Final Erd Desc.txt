The Entity-Relationship Diagram shows the information system's entities and the relationships between those entities. SM Hotels business process works 24 hours a day. Each day has 3 shifts, every shift one of the duty inspector will inspect the scheduled facilities. The duty inspectors are the hotel securities, engineers, and duty managers. Duty inspector will assign more than one schedule and every date in the schedule has several duty inspectors. Facilities can be inspected in different schedule and one date in schedule may have different facilities. Checklist may assign to several facilities but facilities may only have one checklist assigned.
