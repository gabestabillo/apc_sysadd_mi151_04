The Conceptual Framework captures something real and do this in a way that is easy to remember and apply. The diagram shows that the proposed system will be synchronized. The assigned schedule, facilities, and checklist will be stored in H2H Application. The duty inspector can access the H2H Application to see and review all the assigned schedule and facilities to inspect. Also, the H2H Application can provide the checklist for each facility and use it to inspect. After inspection, the duty inspector report will be gather by H2H Application and generate and result of all data gathered.
