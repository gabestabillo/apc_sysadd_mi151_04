The Use Case Diagram shows the interactions among the elements of the system and also shows the actors involved with the system defined according to their roles. This  Use Case Diagram involves 4 actors which are the Front Desk, Duty Manager, Duty Inspector, and the Quality Assurance Department. The role of the Front Desk is to send advise to the Duty Manager. The role of the Duty Manager is to assign an inspector, execute the inspection, and generate a report. The role of the Duty Inspector is to execute the inspection and generate a report. The role of the Quality Assurance Department is to receive the generated report by the Duty Manager and Duty Inspector. The Quality Assurance Department will produce the checklist and QR code to start another inspection.
